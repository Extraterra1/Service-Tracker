# TV JustDrive Brand Design

## Goal

Make the hidden `#tv` operations board feel recognizably JustDrive while keeping the next delivery and recolha effortless to read from across the room.

## Visual direction

- Use the existing JustDrive logo asset at the top-right, without a header bar, card, shadow, or decorative container.
- Give the delivery field a pale JustDrive blush and the recolha field a quieter warm gray.
- Use the logo's red (`#fe3a4f`) for section labels, the flight-time source, and the horizontal division between the two service fields.
- Keep primary times and uppercase client names in a deep warm gray for maximum contrast.
- Preserve Barlow, the 70/30 delivery-to-recolha split, and the current information hierarchy.

## Logo placement

The full existing `Logo Base.svg` wordmark sits absolutely in the top-right of the board. At 961 × 541 it is approximately 150px wide, remains clear of the centered service content, and scales down on narrower layouts.

## Accessibility and resilience

- The logo has the existing descriptive JustDrive alternative text.
- Brand color does not carry operational meaning by itself; labels and hierarchy remain explicit.
- The board must remain exactly viewport-sized with no overflow at 961 × 541.
- Loading and empty states keep the logo and branded surface treatment.
