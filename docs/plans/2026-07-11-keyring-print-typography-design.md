# Porta-chaves Print Typography Design

## Goal

Improve the keyring insert's logo balance and replace generic Helvetica/Arial typography with Sora SemiBold.

## Design

The JustDrive logo will grow from 30 mm to 34 mm wide and be vertically centered inside the upper artwork zone of each plate cell. Plate and phone text will use a locally bundled Sora SemiBold font embedded directly in the PDF, preserving offline generation and consistent printing. Font sizes will be tuned to retain safe side margins in every cell.

The browser preview will use the same Sora family and revised logo proportions. Automated layout tests will protect the new dimensions, and a generated A4 PDF will be rendered to PNG to verify centering, legibility, clipping, and physical layout.
